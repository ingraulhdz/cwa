FAQ's

You’ve got questions, we’ve got answers.

May I drop off my car the day before my scheduled appointment ?

Yes, your car is safely locked in our secure garage overnight. We prefer to wash & prep your car the day before which allows our technicians to start work on your car early the next morning and have it ready by end of day depending on condition. Drop off times are preferred the day before anytime before 3:00 pm if possible. If not you can always schedule for a morning drop off at 8 am.

Is there an extra charge for SUVs , Vans, Exotic and Classic cars?

Prices are higher due to the extra work and attention required for these vehicles. A specific estimate will be provided upon inspection of your car the day of your appointment..

How does your pickup and delivery service work?

We have been offering pickup and delivery service since the initiation of Pro Touch . Any location within 5 miles from our facility includes this complimentary in a Complete Deluxe Detaill, Full Auto Interior Detail, or Full Exterior Auto Detail. Any location outside of 5 miles is a flat rate of $20.00 within the Greater Grand Rapids Area. We are very flexible with these types of appointments, so be sure to let us know what will work best for you and we will do our best to accommodate.

Our vehicle has never been professionally detailed and we are selling it. Can you make it look new again?

We hear this quite often. While having your vehicle detailed just once in its life time right before you sell it will help its first impression on a potential buyer, most likely by this time your vehicle has deteriorated, interior soiled, exterior paint oxidized, and the clear coat damaged.

We can however remove some of the stains inside and polish out most of the oxidation on the exterior paint, but unfortunately your vehicle will never look as nice as if it had received detailing and maintenance in the past. This means you’ll probably get much less than you’re asking for. If the clear coat is damaged or peeling off then the vehicle would have to be repainted. A high quality paint job can cost several thousand dollars as oppose to a few hundred a year to properly maintain your vehicles factory finish.

Not only does having your vehicle detailed hold its value throughout its lifetime, but it also provides a more enjoyable driving experience. A clean vehicle can portray self confidence and a high level of professionalism. It is simply one of the most important maintenance services for your vehicle.

Should I pay before, or after the service has been completed?

We request payment be made upon pickup of the vehicle. We want to ensure that you are completely satisfied with the final product prior to payment. Also – because we perform a full inspection report with estimated cost for cosmetic repair, the payment at the end may be differ due to the possibility for additional approved services.

Do you accept credit cards?

We are able to accept any version of credit card, check or cash either on-the-spot here at ProTouch, or at a location away from our facility. A copy of the receipt can be sent via e-mail, text message, or by postage.
